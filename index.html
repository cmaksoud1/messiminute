Did You Know? 🤯

🏆
19 World Cup Goals

Messi is Argentina's all-time World Cup top scorer with 19 goals across 6 tournaments (2006–2026). Nobody in Albiceleste history comes close.

🔥
7 Straight WC Matches

First player in history to score in 7 consecutive World Cup matches. From the 2022 quarter-final to the 2026 group stage — unstoppable.

⏱️
48 Stoppage Time Goals

Messi has scored 48 goals in stoppage time. The man doesn't believe a match is over until HE says so. Including THAT Inter Miami debut free kick.

📅
91 Goals in 2012

In 2012, Messi shattered Gerd Müller's 40-year record by scoring 91 goals in a single calendar year. A record that may never be broken.

🌍
Every Round of a World Cup

In 2022, Messi became the first player EVER to score in every stage of a single World Cup — group, R16, QF, semi, and final. Complete domination.

6️⃣
6 World Cups

Messi is one of only two players (alongside Ronaldo) to play in 6 World Cups (2006-2026). And he's scored in ALL of them. Longevity + greatness.

🐐
8 Ballon d'Or Awards

Eight. No one else has more than five. Messi won the golden ball more times than most legends won it once. The trophy was made for him.

🇦🇷
123 Goals for Argentina

Argentina's all-time top scorer with 123 international goals in 202 appearances. Surpassed Batistuta's record in 2016 and never looked back.
